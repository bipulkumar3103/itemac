A pressure vessel used in the food manufacturing industry to “commercially sterilize” food after it has been placed into its container and the container has been hermetically sealed

The Water Spray process is also an overpressure process, like water immersion, except that the product is exposed to the influence of the overpressure air. It is similar to the Saturated Steam process in that steam is the driving force for reaching the center of the load. But, the Water Spray process is different from Saturated Steam in that air can be introduced into the vessel during sterilization.
